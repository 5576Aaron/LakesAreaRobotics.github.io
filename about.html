About Us

We are Iowa City Robotics Team 167. Our team was formed in 1998. Our team is comprised of students 9-12 grade from West High, City High, and Regina. There are about 40 members of our team are welcome to all kinds of students. We are part of the First Robotics Challenge, and we have participated in multiple competitions in the past. Last year, 2016, we participated in 2 competitions and got to the quarterfinals in one and the semifinals in the other. The year before, 2015, that we took place in two competitions and made it to the playoffs in both.
