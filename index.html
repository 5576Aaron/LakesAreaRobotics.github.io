About Us

Iowa City Robotics Team 167 was the first robotics team in the state of Iowa. We are a group of young people interested in the STEM fields who work together to build a fully functional robot in just 6.5 weeks. In the past we have had a lot of success including placing 3rd and 5th in regional events with over 60 teams, and qualifying to the FIRST Robotics World Championships.

Sponsors

Team 167 would not be able to function without the support of our sponsors. They have helped to inspire futures in STEM careers and spread awareness about STEM in our community.
