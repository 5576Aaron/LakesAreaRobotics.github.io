Join Our Team

STUDENTS

Our team is made up of students from the Iowa City area. We have members from City High School, Liberty High School, and West High School. We welcome students from 9th to 12th grade. Contact us at iowacityrobotics167@gmail.com if you are interested in joining our team.

MENTORS

Our team has professional engineers and specialists in STEM fields that volunteer as mentors. These include teachers from the Iowa City Community School District, engineers from Collins Aerospace, and more. Contact us if you are interested in mentoring our team.

MEETINGS

Team 167 holds meetings at the Kirkwood Regional Center on Oakdale Blvd. in Coralville. We meet every Tuesday and Thursday from 5:30 to 8:30 p.m. during the preseason (September-December). During the build season (January-April), we meet on Tuesday and Thursday from 5:30 to 8:30 p.m., Saturday from 9:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m., and Sunday from 11:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. In the offseason (May-August), we meet every Thursday from 5:30 to 7:00 pm. Check our team calendar for an updated meeting schedule.

COMPETITIONS

We typically compete in two regional competitions each year. We attend competitions in Iowa and surrounding states. The world championship is held in Detroit, Michigan. We last qualified in 2018.
