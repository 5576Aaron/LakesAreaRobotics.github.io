Get Involved

STUDENTS

Our team is made up of students from the Iowa City area. We currently have members from City High School, Prairie High School, and West High School. We welcome all students from 9th to 12th grade. Fill out this form or contact us if you are interested in joining our team.

MENTORS

Our team has professional engineers and specialists in STEM fields that volunteer as mentors. These include teachers from the Iowa City Community School District, engineers from BAE Systems, and more. Contact us if you are interested in mentoring our team.

MEETINGS

We hold meetings at the Kirkwood Regional Center on Oakdale Blvd. in Coralville. During the fall of 2021, we are meeting on Mondays and Thursdays from 6:30 to 8:00 p.m. For our updated meeting schedule, check our team calendar.

COMPETITIONS

We typically compete in two regional competitions each year. We attend competitions in Iowa and surrounding states. The world championship is held in Detroit, Michigan. We last qualified in 2020.

OUTREACH
